## 1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs all use of emergex software, services, and collective intelligence systems operated by the 8GI Foundation. It complements the Apache License 2.0 under which the emergex source code is distributed.

The 8GI Constitution establishes that "Intelligence should be shared. Interfaces should be personal." This AUP ensures that shared intelligence is not weaponized against the people it exists to serve.

All emergex products - emergex OS, emergex Code, emergex App, emergex World, emergex Games, and emergex Jr - are subject to this policy.

---

## 2. Prohibited Uses

You may NOT use emergex software or services to:

### 2.1 Harm Children
Generate, distribute, or facilitate child sexual abuse material (CSAM), exploit minors, groom minors, or circumvent child safety measures. emergex Jr is the moral center of this ecosystem - any use that endangers children is the most serious violation possible.

*Constitutional basis: Article III (Security by Default), Article VI (Accessibility Is Architecture), Value 03 (Accessibility Is Architecture)*

### 2.2 Develop Weapons
Design, develop, or deploy weapons, weapons systems, or military applications. This includes autonomous weapons, chemical, biological, or nuclear weapons guidance, targeting systems, and any software intended to cause physical harm to people.

### 2.3 Conduct Surveillance
Perform mass surveillance, unauthorized monitoring of individuals, or deploy tracking systems without informed consent. This includes facial recognition for law enforcement without judicial oversight, location tracking without consent, and communications interception.

*Constitutional basis: Article III (Security by Default), Value 04 (Data Sovereignty)*

### 2.4 Manipulate or Deceive
Generate content intended to manipulate, coerce, defraud, or deceive individuals. This includes deepfakes for harassment, social engineering attacks, impersonation, phishing, and disinformation campaigns.

*Constitutional basis: Value 02 (Competence Without Pretense), Article II (Evidence Over Vibes)*

### 2.5 Violate Human Rights
Facilitate torture, forced labor, human trafficking, or any violation of the Universal Declaration of Human Rights.

### 2.6 Discriminate
Build systems that unlawfully discriminate based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, age, or other protected characteristics. This includes discriminatory scoring, profiling, or access-gating systems.

*Constitutional basis: Value 03 (Accessibility Is Architecture), Article VI (Accessibility Is Architecture)*

### 2.7 Compromise Privacy
Collect, process, or exfiltrate personal data without lawful basis and informed consent. This includes violations of GDPR, CCPA, or equivalent data protection laws. User data belongs to users - not to you.

*Constitutional basis: Value 04 (Data Sovereignty), Article III (GDPR and Children's Data provisions)*

### 2.8 Undermine Democratic Processes
Interfere with elections, generate political disinformation at scale, suppress voter participation, or manipulate public discourse through automated means.

### 2.9 Cause Disproportionate Environmental Harm
Deploy emergex systems at a scale intended to cause disproportionate environmental damage without mitigation measures.

### 2.10 Circumvent Safety Measures
Deliberately remove, disable, or bypass NemoClaw policies, safety filters, content moderation, approval gates, or other protective measures built into emergex software for the purpose of enabling any prohibited use listed above.

*Constitutional basis: Article III (Security by Default - default-deny policy engine, approval gates)*

## 3. Collective Intelligence Terms

The emergex ecosystem provides collective intelligence services including the Tool Shed (shared tool registry), pattern sharing, and hive mind capabilities. Access to these services is conditional on AUP compliance.

- **Compliance required.** Access to Tool Shed, pattern sharing, and hive mind intelligence requires ongoing AUP compliance.
- **Violation consequences.** Violation of this AUP results in immediate revocation of collective intelligence access.
- **Local harness unaffected.** Your local emergex installation continues to function as open-source software under the Apache 2.0 license, but without collective intelligence services.
- **The moat is gated by the Constitution.** The value of the collective grows with trust. The Constitution is the gatekeeper.

## 4. Enforcement

### 4.1 Reporting and Investigation
- Violations are reported to the emergex Governance Officer (8GO).
- Investigation is conducted by the emergex Security Officer (8SecO) with support from the board as needed.
- All investigations follow due process principles: notice, opportunity to respond, and documented findings.

### 4.2 Graduated Response
Enforcement follows a graduated response model:

1. **Warning** - Written notice of the violation with required corrective action and timeline.
2. **Suspension** - Temporary revocation of collective intelligence access pending corrective action.
3. **Permanent ban** - Permanent revocation of collective intelligence access and removal from all 8GI community spaces.
4. **Legal action** - Referral to appropriate legal authorities where violations constitute criminal conduct.

### 4.3 Immediate Action
Egregious violations - including but not limited to CSAM, weapons development, and human trafficking - result in immediate permanent ban and referral to law enforcement. No warning. No graduated response.

## 5. Reporting Violations

- **Report to:** security@emergex.dev
- **Anonymous reporting:** Supported. You do not need to identify yourself.
- **Whistleblower protection:** Good-faith reports of AUP violations are protected. The 8GI Foundation will not retaliate against individuals who report violations in good faith, even if the report is ultimately unsubstantiated.
- **Response time:** The 8SecO will acknowledge reports within 72 hours and provide a substantive response within 14 days.

## 6. Changes to This Policy

- This AUP may be updated by board resolution of the 8GI Foundation.
- Material changes will be communicated with at least 30 days notice via the emergex.world website and relevant community channels.
- Continued use of emergex collective intelligence services after the notice period constitutes acceptance of the updated AUP.
- The Apache 2.0 license grant for the open-source code is not affected by AUP changes - only collective intelligence access is conditional.
